Essential Health Precautions

Pre-Workout Safety

Always warm up for 5-10 minutes before intense exercise. Check your heart rate and ensure proper hydration. Consult a doctor if you have any cardiovascular conditions.

Exercise Form

Maintain proper form to prevent injuries. Start with lighter weights and gradually increase intensity. Never sacrifice form for heavier weights.

Rest & Recovery

Allow 48-72 hours between training the same muscle groups. Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep for optimal recovery and muscle growth.

Hydration

Drink water before, during, and after workouts. Aim for at least 8-10 glasses daily, more if you're active or in hot weather.

Warning Signs

Stop exercising immediately if you experience chest pain, dizziness, shortness of breath, or severe fatigue.

Heat Safety

Exercise in cooler parts of the day. Wear breathable clothing and take breaks in hot weather.

Medical Checkup

Get regular health checkups, especially if you're over 40 or have pre-existing conditions.

Supplements

Consult healthcare providers before taking any supplements. Focus on whole foods first.

Nutrition Guidance

Macronutrients

Proteins: 0.8-1.2g per kg body weight for general health, 1.6-2.2g for muscle building.
Carbohydrates: 45-65% of total calories for energy.
Fats: 20-35% of total calories for hormone production.

Meal Timing

Pre-workout: Eat 1-3 hours before with carbs and protein.
Post-workout: Consume protein within 2 hours of training.
Overall: Eat every 3-4 hours to maintain energy levels.

Food Quality

Choose whole, unprocessed foods. Include plenty of vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats like nuts and olive oil.

Portion Control

Use your hand as a guide: palm-sized protein, cupped hand of carbs, thumb-sized fats, and fist-sized vegetables per meal.
